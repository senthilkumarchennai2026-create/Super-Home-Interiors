Our Story

15 Years. 1,000 Homes. One Unwavering Commitment.

Super Home Interiors was built on a simple belief: every family deserves a home that feels completely, beautifully theirs.

Started by a team of passionate designers in Chennai, we've grown from a small studio to a trusted name for over 1,000 families across OMR, ECR, and beyond. We don't just design rooms; we design the way your family wakes up, gathers, works, and rests.
