The Experience

Light, Depth, and Drama —
Without a Single Word.

A false ceiling does something no piece of furniture or wall colour can: it defines the volume of a room. In Chennai's apartments, where slab heights are often standard, a well-designed gypsum or POP ceiling creates the illusion of space, grandeur, and luxury.

We design ceilings for living rooms, bedrooms, kitchens, and dining areas — integrating LED coves, spotlights, and pendant positions from the start, not as afterthoughts.

False ceiling costs depend on the type (POP, gypsum, grid), room size, and complexity of the design. A standard living room POP ceiling typically ranges from ₹60–120 per sq ft. We provide an accurate quote after a site measurement visit.

Both have their place. Gypsum boards are faster to install, more moisture-resistant, and better for contemporary designs. POP allows for more intricate ornamental patterns and is preferred for traditional or classic aesthetics. We recommend based on your design direction and budget.

Ideally, false ceiling is done before painting and after electrical rough-in. If your interior work is already complete, we assess the situation and minimise disruption. Contact us to discuss your specific scenario.
